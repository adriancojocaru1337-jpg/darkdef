How to Play

Complete beginner guide to Dark Defense

Build beside the road, counter each threat, survive boss mechanics, and turn every run into permanent Ley power. This guide covers the current Campaign and Endless systems.

First Battle

Your first three actions

  1. Select Archer in the Unit Shop.
  2. Place it on a green tile beside the road. The transparent tower and dashed circle preview its real range. Red tiles are blocked.
  3. Press Start to call Wave 1. Starting later waves quickly grants extra gold and Bonus Score; the reward decays for 30 seconds.

Place towers near corners or long path segments so enemies remain in range longer. Click a placed tower to upgrade, specialize, or sell it.

Resources

Gold, lives, and score

  • Gold: buys towers, upgrades, and specializations. Kills and clears provide more.
  • Lives: normal enemies cost 1 life at the gate; bosses cost 3.
  • Total Score: combat score plus every Bonus Score point.
  • Bonus Score: rewards combos, quick calls, clears, preserved lives, and special effects. It ranks the Endless Bonus leaderboard.

Tower Synergies

Build adjacent combinations

A different tower type in any of the eight neighboring cells grants a bonus. Each neighbor type counts once; duplicates do not stack.

  • Archer nearby - Volley Drill: +8% attack speed.
  • Hunter nearby - Marked Targets: +8% damage.
  • Mage nearby - Arcane Insight: +6% range.
  • Bomb nearby - Shrapnel Craft: +10% splash radius for splash towers.

Dotted links appear during placement and when selecting a linked tower. Clusters gain power but sacrifice coverage.

Spell Usage

Save spells for danger

  • Frost Nova: 18s cooldown. Slows enemies in an area by 55% for 3.2 seconds.
  • Meteor Strike: 24s cooldown. Deals 180 area damage.
  • Chain Lightning: 16s cooldown. Hits up to 4 nearby targets for 90 damage.

Click a ready spell, then click its target area. Hold spells for leaks, boss ability windows, or overlapping packs.

Boss Mechanics

React to the special ability

  • Root Grasp: snares the nearest tower for 3.5 seconds. Spread damage across several positions.
  • Blood Rage: restores health and gains speed. Save burst damage and control for the rage.
  • Grave Summons: creates fast minions. Keep splash damage near the main path.
  • Void Bulwark: restores health through a shield. Hold Meteor or heavy damage for it.

The boss warning shows the exact mechanic before combat. Endless boss waves bring two bosses and both mechanics together.

Legendary Auras

Boss rewards stay with a tower

  • Inferno Core: burn damage and an explosion on kill.
  • Frost Crown: slow and freeze after repeated hits.
  • Storm Sigil: chain jumps or bonus single-target damage.
  • Golden Pact: bonus gold, Bonus Score, and Pact Surge.

Choose an aura after defeating a boss, then click one tower to bind it permanently for that run.

Campaign & Reserve

Carry your army between stages

Defeat the stage boss to continue. Towers move into Reserve and can be placed for free on the next battlefield. Their levels, specialization, aura, and investment are preserved. Stage clears also provide gold, healing, and Ley Crystals.

Complete all six Campaign stages to unlock Endless Mode.

Ley Attunement

Permanent progression

Spend Ley Crystals earned from waves, bosses, victories, and defeats on three permanent branches:

  • Radiance: damage, speed, range, and stronger Ley Stones.
  • Bastion: starting gold, lives, income, and stage healing.
  • Arcana: spell cooldowns, aura rerolls, crystal gain, and larger aura drafts.

Ley Stones empower towers placed directly on them with damage or range.

Combos

Keep the streak alive

A kill within 2.6 seconds extends the combo. Multipliers activate at x5, x10, and x20. Completing a x10+ combo grants Bonus Score. An enemy reaching the gate breaks it without the completion reward.

Endless Mode

Push beyond the Campaign

Waves scale continuously and every tenth wave brings a random boss pair. Best wave, defeated boss pairs, Total Score, and Bonus Score are tracked. The Endless Bonus leaderboard ranks Bonus Score first.

Advanced Strategy

Build efficient kill zones

Use route geometry

Build around corners, long lanes, and overlapping range pockets. A well-positioned basic tower often outperforms a stronger tower with poor uptime. Let splash towers soften groups while long-range towers finish priority targets.

Invest before expanding

Upgrade towers in proven positions before buying weak filler. Add a new tower when coverage breaks down, a counter is missing, or another route segment becomes important.

Prepare for bosses

Save gold before boss waves, keep at least one spell ready, and buy for the announced mechanic. Protecting lives also preserves Bonus Score and later-run momentum.

Survive Endless pressure

A stable layout should work while spells are unavailable. Establish reliable damage, add splash and control, then reinforce answers for boss pairs before spending on greedy effects.
