Privacy Policy

Dark Defense uses a small set of account and gameplay data to keep player sessions working, support profiles and leaderboards, and improve the overall game experience.

What We Collect

Account and gameplay data

When you create an account, Dark Defense stores your username, email address, password hash, account creation date, selected crest, and gameplay statistics tied to your profile.

When you play while signed in, the game may store run history, scores, kills, progression milestones, and leaderboard submissions linked to your account.

Public-facing areas such as leaderboards and public profiles can display your username, crest, scores, progression highlights, and recent run information.

Sessions

Essential cookies and local storage

Dark Defense uses essential session cookies to keep players signed in securely and to protect account access across pages.

These cookies are required for account login, profile access, password reset flows, and correct leaderboard attribution while signed in.

The game may also use browser storage for convenience features such as interface state, selected player name fallback, and lightweight gameplay preferences.

How It Is Used

Why this data exists

Data is used to authenticate players, preserve long-term identity, power profiles and leaderboards, save gameplay-related progress, and support account management features.

Dark Defense does not use marketing cookies in the current version. If non-essential analytics or advertising tools are added later, the policy and any required consent flow should be updated accordingly.

Player Control

Your choices

You can stop using account features at any time by logging out. You can also update account details from the account page.

You can permanently delete your account from the account page. This removes the account, profile, active sessions, and linked leaderboard or run data associated with that identity.
